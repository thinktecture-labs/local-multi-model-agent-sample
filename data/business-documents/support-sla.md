---
title: Support Tiers and SLAs
category: support
---
Nextera support tiers by plan: Starter: community Discord, documentation, no guaranteed SLA. Professional: email support, 8-hour response SLA (business hours), access to private support portal, monthly office hours. Enterprise: 24/7 phone + dedicated Slack channel, 1-hour response SLA, proactive monitoring, quarterly business reviews, dedicated CSM. All plans include access to the public documentation portal and changelogs. Critical security patches are delivered to all plans within 24 hours.
