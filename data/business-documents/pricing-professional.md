---
title: Professional Plan Pricing
category: pricing
---
The Nextera Professional plan is €999 per month (or €9,990 per year). It includes: up to 25 concurrent users, 100 GB vector storage, models up to 70B parameters, fine-tuning support (LoRA), priority email support with 8-hour SLA, audit logs, SSO via SAML 2.0, and access to the Nextera API. Recommended for production deployments at mid-size companies.
