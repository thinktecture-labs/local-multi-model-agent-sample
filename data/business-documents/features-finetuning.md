---
title: Fine-Tuning Capabilities
category: features
---
Nextera Professional and Enterprise plans include on-device fine-tuning. Supported methods: LoRA, QLoRA (4-bit quantized LoRA), and full fine-tuning for models under 7B. The platform automatically collects interaction logs that can be used as training data, with a one-click fine-tuning pipeline. Typical fine-tuning time: 30–60 minutes for 500 examples on a single A100. Fine-tuned adapters are automatically deployed to Ollama and versioned. GGUF export is supported for deployment to edge devices.
