---
title: Nextera Platform Overview
category: product
---
Nextera Platform is a local-first AI infrastructure product that lets enterprises run large language models, vector search, and agentic pipelines entirely on their own hardware — no data ever leaves their network. Nextera ships as a single Docker container and supports Ollama, llama.cpp, and vLLM as inference backends. Key capabilities include RAG pipelines, function-calling agents, embedding search, and a built-in fine-tuning workflow.
