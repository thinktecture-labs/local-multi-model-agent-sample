---
title: "FAQ: Local AI vs Cloud AI"
category: faq
---
Why run AI locally instead of using cloud APIs? Privacy: Your prompts, documents, and outputs never leave your network. Critical for healthcare, legal, finance, and government use cases. Cost: After initial hardware, inference is free. A team of 50 running 100 queries/day would spend ~€5,000/month on cloud APIs but €0 on local inference after setup. Latency: Local inference on a modern GPU achieves <200ms first token, vs 500–2000ms for cloud APIs under load. Reliability: No rate limits, no outages from provider incidents. Control: Fine-tune models on your proprietary data without sharing it.
